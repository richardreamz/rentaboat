Description: This 23' Pontoon Boat is great for picnics and family outings on Mission Bay. Holds up to 12 passengers (with a max. of 2650 lbs) with padded seats, table, a cooler and bimini top.
Facility Area & Attractions: We have 3 convenient locations in San Diego, with a fleet of more than 200 vessels, sailboats from 16 to 36 ft, powerboats from 15 to 26 ft, jetskis, fishing skiffs, kayaks, paddleboats.Climb aboard one of our boats at our Downtown or Coronado location and you will be amazed how much you can see in just a few hours. Cruise past the Star of India, the oldest merchant ship afloat. Just a bit further and the beautiful San Diego skyline is in full view. Then Harbor and Shelter Island, the U.S. Navy marine base and Point Loma are on your right. Keep a sharp lookout for dolphins, sea lions, and maybe even a whale spout. Head back toward the downtown skyline and on your right you'll see the Naval Air Station, an aircraft carrier or two, and the magnificent Coronado Bay Bridge off in the distance. You can valet dock at our Coronado location and it's just a short walk to the world famous Hotel Del Coronado. Mission Bay and Coronado locations have a bait, tackle and convenient store.
