Area and Attractions:

A typical example of a full day charter on “Mio Amore” (Sunseeker Manhattan 64) – maximum 12 guests. The charter would typically leave from Pontoon B, Grand Harbour, Birgu at 10am cruising out of The Medieval port town of Birgu a 500 year old city, along the coastal line, with panoramic views, stopping at the Blue Lagoon, where you have time to relax on the boat, swim & Snorkel in the crystal clear waters. Enjoy lunch with beautiful surroundings. You would then cruise back into the Spectacular Grand Harbour (one of the largest and deepest Harbours in the world) at around 6pm. Take off your shoes, kick back and simply enjoy! Included: Full captain and stewardess service aboard • Fuel for a 30 Nautical miles round trip, (Any further distances will be charged for according to our fuel fact sheet and log) • Service of a typical light Mediterranean lunch, please let me know if you have any dietary requirements. • Drinks (Water, Wine, Beer & juice) • Inclusive of all insurance fees Toys & Amenities • Pool noodles for use • Snorkels, fins & masks, with glass bottom Kayaks. • Jet rib tender for transporting guests to shore and Cave tours All the above is included in the daily rate of €4200 low season or €4620 high season (July, August and September) We have just recently won at the International Boat & yacht awards “Best Malta based charter company” for the 2nd year running “Best customer service” & "Best Brand Presence"
