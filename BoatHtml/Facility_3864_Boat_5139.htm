Boat Description

Join us for a fabulous adventure aboard Tikimata, sailing the beautiful turquoise waters of the Bahamas Abaco Island Chain. Your adventure begins as soon as you come aboard. We know the islands inside and out having spent many years here and can show you some of the best kept secrets the Abaco Islands have to offer. Your adventures will include, diving and snorkelling through the stunning coral reefs, home to the beautiful marine life of the Caribbean. Or perhaps you would prefer to laze on the stunning white sandy beaches with a picnic and a cold beer to cool you down or watch amazing sunsets from the deck of the boat and watch the cosmos role by on the crystal clear night of an Abaco sky. Your week long adventure is entirely flexible, we can do as much or as little as you would like. The choice is entirely yours. Let Tikimata transport you to a different Island location each day, where you can snorkel and sample local island life, you have so many choices. So come and join us in the Abacos, in our opinion one of the most beautiful sailing areas in the world.

Area and Attractions:

The snorkelling in the Abaco in our opinion is some of the best in the world, we do carry snorkel gear on board for all participants. You will see a variety of tropical fish and be able to snorkel on world famous coral reefs. We will be visiting different islands (cays) during your week long adventure, which will give everyone the opportunity to sample some of the local cuisine. We prepare delicious meals for you each day using fresh local produce and some traditional Bahamian dishes You can also eat out if you so wish. The local Conch Fritters are a particular speciality of the Abaco, and are very much enjoyed by our guests.
